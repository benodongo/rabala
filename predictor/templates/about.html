About PC‐HEWS Lake Predictor

Bridging traditional wisdom and modern science for safer fishing communities

Our Mission

PC‐HEWS was created to preserve and enhance the Indigenous weather knowledge of Lake Victoria communities while integrating modern scientific methods to improve prediction accuracy and safety for fishermen.

Improving Accuracy

Combining the best of both knowledge systems

Our Story

PC‐HEWS began as a research project in 2018 when meteorologists working with Lake Victoria fishing communities noticed the remarkable accuracy of traditional forecasting methods passed down through generations.

After documenting over 200 Indigenous forecasting parameters from elders across Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania, our team developed algorithms to systematically analyze these patterns alongside modern weather data.

Today, PC‐HEWS serves over 50 fishing communities with daily predictions that combine both knowledge systems.
